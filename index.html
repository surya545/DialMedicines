Frequently Asked Questions

What is Dialmedicines.com?

Dialmedicines.com is a telephone enabled healthcare and pharmacy store based in delhi NCR catering to only chronic patients. It is a startup founded by doctors and MBA professionals and is supported by prominent doctors and businessman delhi and USA.

Are the medicines genuine?

We buy from the same pharma companies and stockists that big brand retailers buy from and the medicines are 100% genuine.

Will I be charged any delivery fee?

Our services are free of cost.

How long will it take for you to deliver the order?

We typically can deliver the order same day or within 24-48 hours.

Do you also supply medicines for acute conditions? (like fever, headache etc.)?

If you order these medicines at the time of your monthly order, we can supply them. But we cannot supply medicines for any urgent need or health condition.
