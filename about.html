About Us:

We are a team of “Doctors & Pharmacists” with high managerial skills & have decided to contribute back to our society. We have found patients have complaints of getting fake medicines, highly priced & poor services. We are young & dynamic and have been associated with many partners who want to be part of our mission to reduce pain of people by delivering the genuine medicines at discounted price at their doorstep At dialmedicine.com we provide pharmacy services which have been never experienced before. In our busy schedules, locating and going to a medicine store becomes troublesome, often leading to avoidable delays in getting medicines so DialMedicines would come at your home & Serve you just through a call. DialMedicines is telephone enabled healthcare and pharmacy store, having speed and customer satisfaction as its mantra.


Mission:

To educate people, how affordable healthcare can be obtained without compromising on the quality of care. .


Our Vision

To change the dynamics of pharmacy industry, by promoting generic and affordable medicines and making it available to poorest of people in our developing country. To provide a system, where dependent population of country feels independent in receiving healthcare facility.
